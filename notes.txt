a RESTful Express ap should provide for all the CRUD operations

            REST            |       non REST
                            |
GET         /api/users      |       /getUsers
                            |
POST        /api/users      |       /addUsers
                            |
UPDATE      /api/users/:id  |       /updateUsers
                            |
DELETE      /api/users/:id  |       /deleteUsers

REST allows for more efficient Server Side Routing
